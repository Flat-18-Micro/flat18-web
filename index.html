We’ve ditched per-hour billing for two big reasons.

First, different projects have their own ways of counting active hours, which is a headache for us and confusing for you.

Second, per-hour billing feels too restrictive. With our subscription model, you can aim higher with your development goals.
Plus, by pre-paying, you stay in control of your budget and never have to worry about overspending.
